## üéØ Problem Solved

**Challenge**: The data job market faces critical gaps in understanding:
- Market demand fluctuations and which data roles have highest demand
- Salary benchmarking and competitiveness across roles 
- Work structure gaps including remote work options and health insurance availability
- Education accessibility and degree requirement barriers
- Recruitment platform effectiveness and data inconsistencies

**Solution**: Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing 470,000 job postings with comprehensive salary analysis, work condition insights, and trend identification.

**Impact**: Enables HR teams to optimize hiring strategies, helps data professionals benchmark careers, and guides management in workforce planning decisions.

## üìä Data & Methodology

### **Data Sources**
- **Total Dataset**: 470,000 job postings across 2024
- **Role Coverage**: 6 core data positions (Analyst, Scientist, Engineer + Senior levels)
- **Platform Analysis**: LinkedIn, Indeed, BB job boards
- **Timeframe**: January-December 2024 with monthly trend analysis

### **Salary Star Rating System**
- **Methodology**: 1-5 star rating based on median salary relative to $75K-$150K benchmark
- **5 Stars**: Senior Data Scientist ($155.5K), Senior Data Engineer ($146.5K)
- **3 Stars**: Data Engineer ($126K), Data Scientist ($125K) 
- **2 Stars**: Senior Data Analyst ($107K)
- **1 Star**: Data Analyst ($90K)

### **Data Transformation Process**
1. **Salary Analysis**: Processed yearly and hourly compensation ranges across all roles
2. **Work Condition Mapping**: Categorized remote work availability and health insurance benefits
3. **Education Requirements**: Analyzed degree requirement percentages by role
4. **Platform Aggregation**: Consolidated job counts across major recruitment platforms

## üí° Key Business Insights

### üöÄ **Market Trends**
- **Seasonal patterns**: Strong February peak (47K jobs) with significant September-November decline
- **Role hierarchy**: Senior positions command higher salaries but represent smaller job volumes
- **Education impact**: Data Science roles require degrees (93%) more than Engineering roles (53%)
- **Work flexibility gap**: Remote work availability remains low (10-17%) across all data roles

### üí∞ **Compensation Intelligence**
- **Top earners**: Senior Data Scientist ($155.5K) > Senior Data Engineer ($146.5K) > Data Engineer ($126K)
- **Entry vs Senior**: 19% salary premium for Senior Data Analyst over Data Analyst
- **Hourly vs Salary**: Data Engineer roles show strongest hourly compensation at $59/hour
- **Wide ranges**: Salary spans from $15K-$920K indicating diverse market segments

### üåç **Platform & Benefits Analysis**
- **LinkedIn dominance**: Primary platform across all roles with highest job volumes
- **Limited benefits**: Health insurance offered in only 10-19% of positions
- **Employment types**: Full-time dominates (88-94%), minimal contractor opportunities
- **Accessibility barriers**: High degree requirements may limit entry-level talent pool
